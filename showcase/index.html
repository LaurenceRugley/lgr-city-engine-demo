Procedural worlds

Generate a world from a seed — then sculpt it.

Seeded terrain, biomes, scatter and lakes — a full in-browser world editor: raise mountains, paint ground, plant forests, drop in life, save & share. Day/night and weather run over all of it.

Water that flows & carves.

A real shallow-water sim: pour or rain water and it runs downhill, pools, and — with erosion on — carves rivers into the land you sculpted.
